CoffeeAlum

WHERE STUDENTS AND ALUMNI CAN MEET OVER COFFEE

Our mission is simple. We want to give college undergraduates the opportunity to meet alumni and upperclassmen who can offer invaluable experiential advice about what paths are out there. This can range from insights into what the best classes are to which majors offer the best jobs.

STUDENT EXPLORATION

The number of career opportunities for college students is almost endless. CoffeeAlum makes exploring those avenues easier by connecting students with alumni.

Choosing a career is hard. Coffee isn't.

ALUMNI WISDOM

Take part in the construction of a vibrant community of students and alumni. At your own convenience, you can talk with undergraduates that can probably learn a thing or two from you.
